== Description ==
Do you have a WordPress website for your restaurant? You can now easily direct customers from your website to your restaurant page on [Country website] where they can place an order. You have a choice between a Regular button and a Free Delivery button.

For more details on Takeaway.com please visit https://takeaway.com

== Installation ==

1. Log in to your WordPress Dashboard
1. In the left panel, click 'Plugins', then click 'Add New'.
1. Click ‘Upload Plugin’ then ‘Browse’ and choose the zip-file you downloaded earlier.
1. Click the 'Install Now' button.
1. Wait for the installation to finish, then click the 'Activate Plugin' button.
1. In the left panel, click 'Settings', then click 'Takeaway.com'
1. Change your domain and url to match your restaurant page on Takeaway.com
1. Choose the desired style and color for your widget. A preview is shown at the bottom of your screen.
1. In the left panel, click 'Appearance', then click 'Widgets'
1. Click 'Takeaway.com' and select the desired widget-area on your website, then click 'Add Widget'.
1. Congratulations! You have installed the plugin on your website
